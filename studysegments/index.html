Self-Study Segments

In person (even online) teaching does not scale, and we are working to build material you can use for self-study. Every new application you analyze deepens your skill of getting things tested. Our idea is to do yourself, then see how we did. Do similar thing for another, again seeing how we did. Each round of practice either enforces previously learned concepts or adds specific flavors of exploratory testing.

First Exploratory Testing for eprime

Exploratory Testing is the craft of learning well while testing software so that noticing important information about the software is inevitable.

This site is being created in collaboration of a seasoned tester (Maaret) and a newbie tester (Mirja).
