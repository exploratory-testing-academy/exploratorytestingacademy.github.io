Exploratory Testing Academy


Modern Exploratory Testing builds on insight that lead to naming Exploratory testing 35 years ago: there is a particular approach to testing that treats testing as multidisciplinary and learning-centric activity. While it used to be prevalent in the product companies in Silicon Valley, this approach to testing has since its inception been spotted in the wild globally within companies focused on results without wasted effort.

All good testing is exploratory to an extent, but not all testing is modern exploratory testing that centers learning.

This site is a teaching hub for modern exploratory testing.

Exploratory Testing is the art of learning well while testing software so that noticing important information about the software is inevitable.

This site is being created in collaboration of a seasoned tester (Maaret) and a newbie tester (Mirja).
